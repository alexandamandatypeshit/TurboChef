---
title: Garlic Aioli
section: Sauces
prep_time: 10 mins
cook_time: 0 mins
servings: 6
tags:
  - sauce
  - garlic
ingredients:
  - 2 egg yolks
  - 1 cup neutral oil (sunflower or canola)
  - 1 large garlic clove, minced
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - salt to taste
steps:
  - Whisk egg yolks with lemon and garlic.
  - Slowly drizzle in oil while whisking to emulsify.
  - Season with salt and chill before serving.
