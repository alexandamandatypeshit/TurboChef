---
title: Simple Syrup & Glaze (Nappage léger)
lang: en
section: Sauces
prep_time: 2 mins
cook_time: 2 mins
servings: ~60g
tags:
  - syrup
  - glaze
  - pastry
ingredients:
  - 30g sugar (~2 tbsp)
  - 30g water (~2 tbsp)
steps:
  - Put sugar and water in a small saucepan.
  - Bring to a gentle simmer.
  - Let bubble 1–2 minutes until fully dissolved.
  - Remove from heat and let cool slightly (warm is fine).
notes:
  - No need to thicken; it should stay light and fluid.
  - Brush immediately after baking, while pastries are hot.
  - Use a light brush—don’t soak them.
layout: layouts/recipe.njk
