---
title: Brioche Cinnamon Buns
lang: en
section: Baking
prep_time: 30 mins active, 2h total
cook_time: 25 mins
servings: 6 large rolls
tags:
  - brioche
  - cinnamon
  - buns
  - sweet
ingredients:
  - Dough:
    - 250g flour
    - 40g sugar
    - 1 tsp salt
    - 2¼ tsp active dry yeast (1 packet / 7g)
    - 120g warm milk (105–110°F)
    - 1 egg
    - 75g very soft butter (30% of flour weight)
  - Filling:
    - 50g soft butter
    - 75g brown sugar
    - 2 tsp cinnamon
  - Glaze (optional):
    - 60g powdered sugar
    - 1–2 tbsp milk
steps:
  - Bloom yeast: Warm milk + 1 tsp sugar + yeast. Let foam 5–10 min.
  - Mix: Combine flour, remaining sugar, salt. Add yeast mixture and egg. Mix to form dough.
  - Knead: Add soft butter, knead until smooth and elastic (10 min by hand or 5 min mixer).
  - First rise: Cover and let rise until doubled (about 1 hour).
  - Shape: Roll out dough, spread with butter, sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon. Roll up and slice into 6 pieces.
  - Second rise: Place in greased 8x8 pan, cover, rise until puffy (30–45 min).
  - Bake: 350°F (175°C) for 25 min until golden.
  - Glaze: Mix powdered sugar and milk, drizzle over warm buns.
notes:
  - For extra richness, use all butter and whole milk.
  - Can be prepped the night before and baked in the morning.
