---
title: Gooey Fudgey Brownie
lang: en
section: Baking
prep_time: 10 mins
cook_time: 25 mins
servings: 1 8x8 pan
tags:
  - brownie
  - chocolate
  - fudgy
  - dessert
ingredients:
  - 115g butter (½ cup)
  - 155g white sugar
  - 65g brown sugar
  - 2 large eggs
  - 1 tsp vanilla
  - 75g cocoa powder (¾ cup)
  - 65g flour (½ cup)
  - ½ tsp salt
  - ½ tsp espresso powder (very fine)
  - "Optional: 1 tbsp oil for extra fudginess"
  - "Optional: 1–2 tbsp hot water or hot coffee for blooming"
steps:
  - "Preheat: 325°F (165°C). Line or grease 8x8 pan."
  - "Bloom cocoa + espresso: Melt butter gently. Stir in cocoa powder and espresso powder. (Optional: add hot water or coffee.) Mix until smooth and glossy."
  - "Mix: Add sugars, eggs, and vanilla. Stir until combined."
  - "Add dry: Fold in flour and salt until just combined. Do not overmix."
  - "Bake: Pour into pan. Bake 25 min or until just set in center. Cool before slicing for best texture."
notes:
  - For extra gooey brownies, slightly underbake.
  - Add chocolate chips or nuts if desired.

---
